# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Perplexed Horse community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or personal characteristics.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate
- Constructive feedback and collaboration
- Clear, professional communication
- Focusing on the technical merits of ideas

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behaviour
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behaviour
- Taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable conduct
- Acting in good faith to maintain a professional environment

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:

- Within project spaces
- In public spaces when representing the project or organisation

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the organisation maintainers.

Maintainers may take actions including:

- Warning
- Temporary restriction
- Permanent ban from the organisation
